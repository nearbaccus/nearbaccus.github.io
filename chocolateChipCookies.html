Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

1 c salted butter (2 sticks), room temperature

3/4 c white sugar

1 c brown sugar

2 large eggs, room temperature

1 t vanilla

3 cups all-purpose flour

3/4 t salt

3/4 t baking soda

2 1/2 cups Ghirardelli chocolate chips, (plus optional 1/2 c more)

Directions

Cream together butter and sugars on a medium-low speed. Add vanilla, eggs, and beat just until thoroughly combined. Add salt, soda, and flour all at once and mix at a medium-low speed until combined. Stir in chocolate chips. Form heaping tablespoons, by hand, into rough, round balls. Bake at 350 for 8 to 10 minutes. You do not want to brown the cookies, just cook until set around the edges.
