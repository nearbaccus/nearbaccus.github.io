Winter Balsamic Vinaigrette Salad

Ingredients

3 T best quality extra virgin olive oil

1 T balsamic vinegar

1 t sugar

Pour all ingredients into a mason jar; cover and shake vigorously.

Directions

1. Serve over salad:

2. Mixed greens

3. Feta cheese, crumbled

4. Green apples, diced in 1/4 pieces

5. Roasted pecans

6. Craisins to taste, optional

Layer all ingredients, starting with mixed greens. Pour dressing over top right before serving.
